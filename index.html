Everest Yang

I am a CS student at Brown University, advised by Prof. George Konidaris. I was honored to receive the 2026 Barry M. Goldwater Scholarship.

I also work with Prof. Igor Shuryak at Columbia University Irving Medical Center on computational oncology. Previously, I interned at Netflix, Anduril Industries, Zeta Surgical, AWS, and NASA.

I am broadly interested in robot manipulation, world models, and reinforcement learning. My research goal is to build general-purpose policies that enable robots to act robustly in the real world.
