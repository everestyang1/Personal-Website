About Me

Hi, my name is Everest Yang, and I'm from Massachusetts. I am an undergrad student at Brown University pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science (Tracks: AI/ML & Systems).

After graduating, I am planning to pursue a PhD in Computer Science/Robotics. My interests range from Deep Learning to Surgical Robotics, Oncology, and more.

In my internship at AWS, I developed a Generative AI application with several AWS services (Bedrock, S3, Lambda, Athena, OpenSearch, etc). At NASA, I worked on wireless networking infrastructure to establish Wi-Fi and cellular communication (3GPP) on the Moon.

In my free time, I enjoy playing tennis, hiking, trying new foods, and spending time with my friends and family.
