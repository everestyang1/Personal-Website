About Me

Hi, my name is Everest Yang, and I’m from Massachusetts. I am currently an undergraduate student at Brown University pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science (Tracks: AI/ML & Systems).
After graduating, I am planning to pursue a M.S. in Computer Science and aspire to be a Software Engineer. My interests range from Deep Learning to Cloud Computing, Systems, and more.

In my previous internship at NASA, I worked on wireless networking infra to establish Wi-Fi and cellular communication (3GPP) on the Moon. Specifically, my team is developing lunar surface communication systems between spacesuits, the rover, and other EVAs. You can read more in my Experience or Blog sections!

In my free time, I enjoy playing tennis, hiking, trying new foods, and spending time with my friends and family.
